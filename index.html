Real-Time 3D Tracking and AI Analysis for Squash

Leveraging existing data from online sources, AI models can be trained to discern patterns in squash gameplay, enhancing their understanding of data intricacies.

Real-time analysis empowers models to process live match data, facilitating immediate insights for analysis, which can be used to improve spectator and referee live experience.

AI models can analyze gameplay, aiding referee decisions, generating tactical insights, and enriching the spectator experience through enhanced viewing capabilities.

Abstract

In an innovative leap for squash enthusiasts and professionals alike, our project harnesses cutting-edge AI technology to revolutionize how player movements and poses are analyzed during matches. By meticulously tracking and translating these dynamics into detailed 3D avatars, we offer an unparalleled view into the gameâ€™s intricacies. This system not only enriches the audience's comprehension of player strategies and motions but also equips referees with powerful tools to enhance decision-making accuracy in near real-time. Beyond offering vivid visualizations, such as hit heatmaps, our technology delves deep into game analytics, providing precise statistics on player hits and winning tactics. Designed for seamless integration into live broadcasts and online platforms, our project aims to demystify the fast-paced action of squash, making it more accessible and engaging for fans and professionals.
