Abstract

In an innovative leap for squash enthusiasts and professionals alike, our project harnesses cutting-edge AI technology to revolutionize how player movements and poses are analyzed during matches. By meticulously tracking and translating these dynamics into detailed 3D avatars, we offer an unparalleled view into the game’s intricacies. This system not only enriches the audience's comprehension of player strategies and motions but also equips referees with powerful tools to enhance decision-making accuracy in near real-time. Beyond offering vivid visualizations, such as hit heatmaps, our technology delves deep into game analytics, providing precise statistics on player hits and winning tactics. Designed for seamless integration into live broadcasts and online platforms, our project aims to demystify the fast-paced action of squash, making it more accessible and engaging for fans and professionals. As part of our website and project promotion, we invite you to explore how we're setting new standards for sports analytics and viewer engagement in squash.
